Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 16, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the Peaceful Flight app for Android, developed by Roman Polach ("we", "us", or "our").

Summary

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data used by the app stays on your device.

What data does the app access?

Accelerometer (Motion Sensor)

The app reads your device's accelerometer to display G-force information. This sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real-time and is not stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Location

The app requests location permission to display local weather information. Your location is sent directly to a third-party weather service to retrieve weather data. We do not store, log, or have access to your location data — it is only used momentarily to fetch weather and is not retained.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services — Privacy Policy
  • Weather API — Location is sent to retrieve weather data. We do not control or store this data.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Any other user data

We have no servers, no databases, and no way to access any information from your device.

Data Storage

The app stores user preferences (such as settings) locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

Since we do not collect any personal data, GDPR data subject rights (access, rectification, erasure, portability) do not apply. There is no data to access, correct, or delete.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
