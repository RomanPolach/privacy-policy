Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 25, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the Peaceful Flight app for Android, developed by Roman Polach ("we", "us", or "our").

Summary

Most data used by the app stays on your device. We do not collect personal information such as your name or email address. However, the app uses Firebase services for crash reporting and app analytics, and limited diagnostic or analytics data may be transmitted to help us improve the app.

What data does the app access?

Accelerometer (Motion Sensor)

The app reads your device's accelerometer to display G-force information. This sensor data is processed entirely on your device in real-time and is not stored, logged, or transmitted anywhere.

Location

The app requests location permission to display local weather information. Your location is sent directly to a third-party weather service to retrieve weather data. We do not store, log, or have access to your location data. It is only used momentarily to fetch weather and is not retained by us.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services - Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics - Used to collect crash reports and technical diagnostics so we can fix problems and improve app stability.
  • Firebase Analytics - Used to collect basic app usage and diagnostics information to help us understand and improve the app.
  • Weather API - Location is sent to retrieve weather data. We do not control or store this data.

Data Collection

We do not directly collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, etc.)
  • Messages, photos, or contacts
  • Any data you do not choose to share through the app's features

However, the app does use Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics. These services may collect technical information such as crash logs, device type, operating system version, app version, and general app interaction data. We use this information only to monitor app performance, fix bugs, and improve the app.

We do not run our own servers or databases for this app. Data collected for crash reporting and analytics is processed by Firebase on our behalf.

Data Storage

The app stores user preferences (such as settings) locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are in the European Union, limited technical and analytics data may be processed through Firebase as described above. If you have questions about this processing, please contact us and we will do our best to help.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
